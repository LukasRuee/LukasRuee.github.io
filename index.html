About Me

I'm a passionate game developer currently studying at the SAE Institute. I specialize in Unity and Unreal Engine, focusing on gameplay mechanics, game optimization, and AI development.
During my time at SAE, I’ve worked on several solo projects, ranging from 2D platformers to 3D adventure games. My goal is to continue honing my skills and contribute to innovative game projects in the industry.

Technologies I use: Unity, Unreal Engine, C#, C++, Blender, and more.

Some of my prototypes and project are not uploaded yet on my github, but will be soon.
